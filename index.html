Kyle E. Mitchell’s CB1100

The 2014 Honda CB1100 motorcycle with VIN JH2SC6517EK100572 belongs to Kyle E. Mitchell.

The bike calls Oakland, California home.

Advices

Pop the seat and clean the hidden parts of the rear fender from time to time. Even if you garage the bike, you will end up with rust spots from condensation.

Spray the rubber grommets that hold the side covers to the top of the frame with silicone lubricant before reinstalling.
